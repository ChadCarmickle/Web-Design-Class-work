Story Characters behind the Scene Special Thanks

Rachel

the first girl is known as Rachel. She grew up in a poor family in Florida and had to make everything count from the very beginning, she is a stubborn girl who is up front with who she is, she was never picky like her sisters, The hobbies that Rachel enjoyed most was making clothes and sewing, especially for her family who she cares deeply for and often watched over. Though hard work and dedication her parents managed to scrape enough cash together and send her to Arizona state.

Brittney

The next girl known as Brittney grew up is the flat land known as Texas, She was often proud of where she came from known as a texan girl. She would often try to read various novels her favorite were those of western origins. She would often spend her time on the farm her family owned helping around when she could and when times were tough she pulled through to help keep the farm afloat. Along with her acting and dancing she was able to pull some personal cash, enough to afford to attend Arizona state.

Brook

The third girl, brook was a loud character, having grown up in Hawaii she was always afraid of sharks and never dared to swim in the waters fearing them, she never had to worry about eating as her family were wealthy so instead she spent a lot of her time in the kitchen cooking various meals and talking with the chief learning what she could do and help out. She often surprised her parents with meals she had cooked and enjoyed their reaction every time, apart from cooking Brook also loved singing and doing arts and crafts in her down time. When the time came for Brook to attend high school her parents felt that Hawaii was just too dull for their precious little princess, and decided to send her to Arizona state, like her brothers before her did in the past.

Emma

The last girl who was a bit strange and secretive and often shy this girl was Emma, born in a middle family in Canada she always loved going outside and enjoying the nature. She often went out and drew all the various wildlife around her, picturing what it would be like to run free like them and enjoy the breeze and freedom of school homework or worse...gym class. Even though she disliked running she did enjoy the aspect the swimming which she became quite good at. When she become the age of high school her parents decided the cold was too much and moved more south to a warmer state known as Arizona. Since best school around was Arizona state her parents decided it would be best to send their little Brook there.
